Applying 'favourite' status or star ratings are some traditional approaches to assessing the quality of a photo.
Coming back from holiday, I was curious what tools exist to show pairs of photos from a given folder and let me decide which photo is better.
I wanted something that would sort both photos into an overall position within the library, so every photo receives a sort index relative to the other photos.
